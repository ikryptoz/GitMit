# Trademark and Brand Policy

This policy defines how the GitMit name, logo, and branding assets may be used.

## Ownership

The GitMit name, logos, and visual identity are project brand assets.
Source-code license terms do not automatically grant trademark rights.

## Allowed Without Prior Approval

- factual references to GitMit in reviews and tutorials,
- links to official repository and documentation,
- unmodified logo use when referencing the official project.

## Not Allowed Without Written Permission

- publishing a cloned app under the same or confusingly similar name,
- using GitMit logos in a way that implies official partnership/endorsement,
- distributing modified builds while presenting them as official GitMit releases,
- creating deceptive branding intended to impersonate official channels.

## Forks and Derivatives

Forks are allowed by source license terms, but must:
- use distinct app name and package identifiers,
- remove official GitMit trademarked branding,
- clearly state that fork is unofficial.

## Reporting Abuse

If you discover impersonation or trademark misuse, contact maintainers with:
- URL/package listing,
- evidence/screenshots,
- date/time observed.
